Shelby County, TN, Census Tracts: Education Attainment of BA Population 25+ (2022)

The map is a Choropleth map of Shelby County, TN, Census Tracts. The spatial pattern shows that the Census Tracts with the highest population percentage 25 plus with a Bachelors (dark purple) are located in the central and the eastern area of the county. This aligns with the makeup of the county where we find the higher incomes and homes are located. The rest of the county shows more variablity, with a mix of low to high. Shelby County, TN, Census Tracts: Median Household Income (2022)

This is a Graduated Symbol map of Shelby County, TN. It is showing the median household income by Census Tracts. The county encompasses the city of Memphis to the west and suburbs to the east. The spatial pattern of the Median Household Income concludes as expected, the higher median incomes is in the suburbs that is is located in the east side of the county, while the lower median incomes appear in the city, that is in the western part of the county. The middle median incomes are located between the lower and higher median incomes scattered throughout the county.

Shelby County, TN, Census Tracts: Bivariate Choropleth Map

The legend displays nine colors. Each color provides an understanding of the spatial makeup of the two attributes. The white/grayish color at the bottom of the legend is where both attributes are at their lowest, a low percentage of the population 25+ with a bachelor's and the lowest household median incomes. On the map this color appears in the western central part of the county, which is the city, they are spatially contiguous. The outliers in the legend are the colors at the middle far left and right (med blue and red) this indicates that the Household Median Income (high)/percent 25+ with a BA (low) and the reverse percent 25+ population (high)/Household Median Income (low), respectively.

South American Banana Trade Flow Project

The map is a flow map of the South American Trade Flow. The northern countries are in a pink color. The pink color represent those countries with high exports and low imports, while the countries in green represent countries with low exports and high imports. The flow of trade is running from north to south. The northern countries have a climate that is condusive to growing bananas unlike the southern countries, hence the direction of flow. The black circle in the center of each country are nodes. The size is proportional to the total flow in each country (imports + exports).
