Adeola Dokun, GISP GIS Consultant

I have a Bachelor's in Political Science from the City University of New York, Queens College. My professional direction changed after I worked as a Geographic Information Systems (GIS) intern with the East St. Louis Financial Advisory Authority in East St. Louis, IL. This inernsship opened the doors to what is now my profressional career as a GIS Consultant with a GISP certification. I have been in the GIS industry for over 20 years. I have worked in both private and public domains providing spatial analysis in the Transportation, Logistics, Gas & Electric, Demography (domestic and global) fields to name a few. I started my own company taking on contracts to provide GIS consulting services. I have worked for the following companies:

  • Bureau of Land Management, Dept of Interior - Field Mobile Application project
  • Northwest Natural - ESRI Utility Network project
  • New Mexico DoIT - Broadband expansion project
  • PG&E (Pacific Gas & Electric) - Software application projects
  • ECS Federal - USPS GIS template
  • North Carolina State Transportation - Strategic Prioritization initiative
  • S & P Global - Demographic and geographic Content Lead
I have a passion for spatial analysis and mapping because I recognize that it touches many, if not every industry. It povides a visual understanding that tabular data cannot. I also have a passion to provide asset mapping information to the countries in Africa. Understanding how things relate to one another can minimize waste and remove redundancy. It provides a clear picture to hopefully making informed decisions.
